Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 20 July 2026 · Last Updated: 20 July 2026

1. Introduction

Sankirtan ("the App", "we", "us", "our") is a devotional music platform that allows users to browse, save, organize, and perform bhajans. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using Sankirtan, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your Google display name, email address, and profile photo URL. We do not receive or store your Google password.
  • User-Generated Content: Bhajans you add to your personal library, programs/setlists you create, keywords and tags you assign, and feedback you submit through the app.
  • Venue Information: If you enter a venue name when creating a program, this is stored in your account. We do not track your physical location.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Firebase Analytics: We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand general usage patterns (e.g., number of active users, popular features). This may collect device type, operating system, and general interaction data. No personally identifiable information is shared with third parties through analytics.
  • Local Storage: We store cached copies of your bhajan library and programs in your browser's local storage to improve loading speed. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect payment or financial information.
  • We do not collect precise geolocation data.
  • We do not collect contacts, call logs, or SMS data.
  • We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking.
  • We do not collect data from minors knowingly. The App is not directed at children under 13.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your account.
  • Store and sync your personal bhajan library and programs across devices.
  • Display your profile name and photo within the app.
  • Improve the app's performance and user experience through aggregated, anonymized analytics.
  • Respond to feedback you submit.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore) servers. Firebase is SOC 2 and ISO 27001 certified. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.
  • Access to your personal bhajan library is restricted to your authenticated account through Firestore Security Rules.
  • The admin account has access to the public bhajan library and user feedback submissions only. The admin cannot access individual users' personal libraries.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Your data is retained as long as you maintain an active account.
  • You may delete individual bhajans or programs from your library at any time through the app.
  • To request complete deletion of your account and all associated data, contact us at the email provided below. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.
  • Cached data in your browser's local storage can be cleared through your browser settings at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app.
  • Correct your data by editing bhajans, programs, or profile information.
  • Delete your data by removing content from your library or requesting full account deletion.
  • Withdraw consent by signing out and discontinuing use of the app.

If you are located in India, your rights are governed by the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA), and you may exercise your rights as a Data Principal accordingly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
